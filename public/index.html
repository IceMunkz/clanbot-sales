Raid Alerts

Instant Discord ping the moment explosions are detected near your base. Get an FCM push notification even when Rust is closed — never get caught offline-raided again.

Smart Switches

Control your electrical switches — turrets, auto-turrets, lights, traps — directly from Discord buttons. No more running back to base to toggle defence systems.

Market Tracker

Live vending machine prices across the map. Subscribe to items and get alerts the moment something drops below your target price. Full price history included.

Look up monument CCTV camera codes and control drones directly from Discord. Recon without ever leaving the app.
